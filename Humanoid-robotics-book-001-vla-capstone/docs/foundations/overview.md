# Module 0: Foundations of Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to Module 0, where we lay the groundwork for understanding the fascinating world of humanoid robotics. This module will introduce you to the core concepts, historical context, and the current state of the art in physical AI.

## Learning Outcomes

Upon completing this module, you will be able to:

- Define what constitutes a humanoid robot and differentiate it from other robotic forms.
- Identify and describe the key subsystems that make up a humanoid robot.
- Understand the fundamental challenges and opportunities in humanoid robotics.
- Recognize modern examples of humanoid robots and their applications.
- Envision the future trajectory of humanoid robotics and its impact on society.

## Module Structure

This module is divided into the following sections:

1.  **What is Humanoid Robotics?**: Defining the field and its unique characteristics.
2.  **Core Subsystems**: An in-depth look at the essential components like locomotion, manipulation, perception, and cognition.
3.  **Modern Examples**: Showcasing cutting-edge humanoid robots from research and industry.
4.  **Future Trajectory**: Discussing emerging trends, challenges, and the potential impact of humanoid robotics.

Let's begin our journey into the foundations of humanoid robotics!
